## Gallery — shot-list (this is the only capture work you need to do)

Product Hunt shows a gallery of images. Capture these **5 screenshots + 1 GIF**,
in this order. Target size **1270×760** (PH crops to ~16:9; anything close works).
Use a real-looking demo property with good photos — visuals sell this.

| # | Screen | Where in the app | Caption to overlay/use |
|---|--------|------------------|------------------------|
| 1 | **Guest booking page** (the public site a guest sees — hero + calendar + Book button) | A published Propvian site / preview | "A booking site your guests actually trust — real calendar, instant checkout." |
| 2 | **Website builder** (3-panel: sections list / live preview / properties) | `/website` builder | "Build it in minutes. No code — drag sections, pick a template, go live." |
| 3 | **Checkout / payment step** (Stripe or PayPal, showing 'you keep 100%') | Guest booking flow payment step | "Guests pay you directly. 0% booking commission — money hits your own Stripe/PayPal." |
| 4 | **Host dashboard** (reservations, revenue, calendar overview) | `/dashboard` (direct-booking) | "Every direct booking, payment, and guest in one place." |
| 5 | **Calendar** (availability, with an OTA-synced blocked date) | `/calendar` | "One-way iCal sync blocks your Airbnb/Vrbo dates — never double-book." |
| GIF | **10–20s: land on booking page → pick dates → reach checkout** | Screen-record the guest flow | (no caption needed — motion is the hook) |

**First image = the thumbnail.** Make #1 the most visually appealing (great
property photo, clean calendar). It's what people see in the feed.

**Overlay tips:** a thin caption bar at the bottom in the brand indigo (#4F46E5)
with white text reads well and stays consistent. Keep the actual UI the star.

## Launch-day playbook

**When:** Launch at **12:01 AM Pacific**, on a **Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday**
(most traffic, avoids weekend + Monday pileup). Avoid US holidays.

**Before launch (day before):**
- [ ] Gallery images + GIF uploaded, thumbnail set (image #1)
- [ ] Tagline + description + topics filled
- [ ] First comment drafted and ready to paste
- [ ] Line up 8–12 people who'll genuinely try it and leave a real comment (not
      just an upvote — PH weights comments and penalizes obvious vote rings)
- [ ] Make sure signup works end-to-end on mobile (most PH traffic is mobile)

**Launch moment:**
- [ ] Post goes live 12:01 AM PT
- [ ] Paste your maker's first comment immediately
- [ ] Message your supporters that it's live (don't say "upvote me" — say "I
      launched, would love your honest feedback")

**All launch day:**
- [ ] Reply to **every** comment within ~30 min, especially the first 4 hours
- [ ] Share the PH link on your X/LinkedIn and in any communities where you're an
      active, welcome member (not cold spam)
- [ ] Answer host questions with specifics; thank critics, note the feedback

**Rules — do not:**
- ❌ Ask directly for upvotes (against PH rules; they detect and penalize it)
- ❌ Buy upvotes or use vote rings — instant penalty
- ❌ Launch and disappear — engagement in the first hours drives ranking
